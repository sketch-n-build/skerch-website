Sketch n Build

What is it?

sketch n build™ is a Community based Capacity building program, created to help create solutions to peculiar challenges within our various communities, through Technology advancement. This is a product of skaetch, it happens twice in a year and it is basically informed by the need of the host community. Our team of peace loving moderators, facilitators help in ensuring that your satisfaction is fully derived through the period of this exercise. Some call it an internship opportunity, while others calls it a hackathon experience, it is indeed relative based on your understanding of those two words. For the first iteration of 2021, we will be kicking off with Software Engineering using Python.click to learn more
The decision to commence the year 2021 with Python is informed by the robustfulness of the language in addressing a broad spectrum of solutions, ranging from Web Development, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Game development and GUI programming amongst various other solution.
Our approach is simple: Onboard, Train and engage!
To enable us guarantee your engagement after the training, we are partnering with Businesses and stakeholders within the hosting community who will be recipients of the various solutions that will be created and sampled during the demo. This internship is free to attend.
You can join our on-site or online cohort, depending on which of the opening is more comfortable for your timing.
