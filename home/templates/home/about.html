About Us

Who are we?

Skaetch is a team of passionate Technologists and Business developers with vast experiences in their various career line. They came together in the summer of 2019, to look at the various challenges that are preventing youths from reaching their potential goals. It was observed that most times, we focus more on what the other party can do for us than we focus on what we can really do to change the status of the environment where we find ourselves. This has played out in some of the feedback we got while validating our idea - hence, with our little resources, we have decided to contribute to reshaping the future of Africa, one community at a times through Digital Literacy and access to job.

We achieve this through:

  • - Partnership
  • - Training
  • - Bootcamps
  • - Hackathons

What can your Partnership do for us?

The process of learning is not complete without the opportunity to implement and try out what you have learnt. Our Partners can provides resources to help our interns/students to learn the required skills while others provide the platform to implement what they have learnt through internship or outright hire

Our HR experts will work with you to ensure that the skillsets you are looking for is harnessed through the period of the internship
